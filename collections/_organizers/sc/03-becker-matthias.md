    Matthias Becker is an Assistant Professor at KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden). He received his B.Eng. degree in Mechatronics/Automation Systems from the University of Applied Sciences Esslingen, Germany in 2011. In the year 2013, he got his M.Sc. degree in Computer Science specializing in embedded computing from the University of Applied Sciences Munich, Germany. He received his Licentiate and PhD degree in Computer Science and Engineering from MÃ¤lardalen University in 2015 and 2017 respectively. Matthias has been a visiting researcher at CISTER - Research Centre in Real-Time and Embedded Computing Systems in Porto, Portugal for two months in 2015 and for three months in 2016. His research focus is on the design and analysis of real-time embedded systems with a focus on multi- and many-core platforms. His research addresses end-to-end timing analysis of task chains, schedulability analysis, software design for predictability but also the design and analysis of the Network-on-Chip interconnect used in these hardware platforms.
